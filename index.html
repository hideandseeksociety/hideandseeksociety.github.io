EVERY MOMENT COUNTS

For what do we exist if not to seek for glory? As adults we spend so much time being serious and sensible, and forget to nurture our inner child. Simple pleasures, the thrill of the hunt, the exhilaration of the chase, drive us to ever more exciting games. We run informal social events where adults meet to play such classics as Hide and Seek, Grandma’s Footsteps, Splat, and many more. 

Many hands make light work, especially when those are HandS – Hide and Seek societies. Our secondary goal is to spread the joy of Hide and Seek to every region of the UK, assisting students to set up University Students’ Union-approved societies, and providing resource packs and guidance to allow affiliated societies around the UK to flourish and join in our common cause. Not being found. 

We also run fundraising events for children’s charities, with our members voting on which charity to support each year at our virtual AGM.
